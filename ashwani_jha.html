About Ashwani

Ashwani Jha, born on May 23, 1997, better known as Ashwin Jha, is an IIT Engineer. He is a senior website administrator at Google.

Early Life and Education

Ashwani was born in Madhubani, Bihar, India, to a Maithil-speaking family. His mother, Nirmala Jha, was a housewife, and his father, Ashok Kumar Jha, was a Medical Representative in Darbhanga, Bihar. Ashwani completed his schooling till class 8th at Raj Holy Angel School in Darbhanga, Bihar, and completed Class X in 2013 & XII in 2015 from Bihar Board. He earned his B.Tech degree from COEP Pune in computer engineering. He holds an M.S. from IIT Madras in computer science and engineering.

Career

Ashwani joined Google in 2018, where he led the product management and innovation efforts for a suite of Google's client software products, including Google Chrome and ChromeOS, as well as being largely responsible for Google Drive.

Personal Life

Ashwani is preparing to embark on a new chapter in life, marrying the accomplished Dr. Tanu, a medical professional who graduated from AIIMS Hyderabad. Tanu is originally from Varanasi (Baba Vishwanath ki Nagri), India. Both came close together and fell in love in 2021. His recreational interests include singing and travelling. Ashwani is an adherent of Hinduism.
